Mars Exploration

Welcome to NASA’s Mars Rover Program

NASA’s fleet of robotic explorers has transformed our understanding of Mars. From searching for signs of ancient water to studying Martian geology, these rovers push the boundaries of human curiosity.

Featured Mars Rovers

Curiosity Rover

Launched 2011 • Gale Crater

Curiosity explores Mars to determine if the planet ever had conditions suitable for life, studying rock, soil, and the atmosphere for clues about its past.

Learn More

Perseverance Rover

Launched 2020 • Jezero Crater

Perseverance searches for signs of ancient life and collects samples that may one day be returned to Earth.

Learn More

Opportunity Rover

Launched 2003 • Meridiani Planum

Opportunity far exceeded expectations, exploring Mars for nearly 15 years and discovering strong evidence of past water.
